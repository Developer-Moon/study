The dataset is from: https://www.eia.gov/dnav/pet/pet_pri_gnd_dcus_nus_w.htm#

This is the time indexed data for Diesel (On - Highway) - All Types from 1994 - 2021.
The dataset has been cleaned up to remove the header columns and reverse the order of the
observations to be from 1994 - 2021 as opposed to 2021 - 1994 in the original dataset.
